Located at the edge of Belfast, Elite Drive has been a trusted name in luxury and performance cars for over 10 years. Since our founding, we’ve built a reputation for offering an unparalleled selection of the finest vehicles.We pride ourselves on providing an exceptional customer experience, offering expert advice and personalised services tailored to each client’s unique needs. Our team of passionate car enthusiasts and industry professionals are dedicated to ensuring you find the perfect car that fits your lifestyle and desires.

We understand that our customers value a seamless experience, which is why we offer an in-house finance team to assist with all your payment needs. Additionally, we provide drive-away insurance that covers you for the first 3 days. To further enhance peace of mind every vehicle comes with service packages and the manufacturer's warranty, ensuring complete confidence in your purchase.

With over a decade of experience, Elite Drive is committed to continuing its legacy of excellent customer service and helping you drive away in the car of your dreams.

  • 2018

    By 2018, we had significantly expanded our business evolving beyond sales to include comprehensive servicing and valeting for all our premium vehicles. Our team grew from just 4 to an impressive 30, with dedicated departments for marketing, sales, and mechanics. With this growth, we proudly welcomed Mercedes and Porsche into the Elite Drive family.

    Last updated 6 years ago

  • 2022

    By June 2022, we had achieved a remarkable milestone: selling over 8,000 cars and welcoming more than 200,000 potential customers through our doors. Our commitment to excellence was recognised with multiple awards, including Outstanding Customer Service and Belfast’s Best Employer of the Year for 2022. All this success did not go unnoticed, as it paved the way for us to proudly welcome the iconic Italian duo of Ferrari and Lamborghini into our showroom. Yes you read that right! We couldn’t believe it either when we received the email expressing their interest to do business.

    Last updated 2 years ago

  • 2024

    In 2024, we celebrated welcoming our 100th team member and expanded our services further by introducing a state of the art tyre repair centre within our showroom. All our technicians have earned official manufacturer accreditations, ensuring they are fully qualified to carry out repairs on premium vehicles. Looking ahead, we are proud to announce that by the summer of 2025, the prestigious Bugatti and Koenigsegg will join the Elite Drive family, further solidifying our position as a leader in luxury automotive sales and service.
