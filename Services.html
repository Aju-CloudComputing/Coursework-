Services

MOT Check

At Elite Drive, we understand that your supercar deserves the highest level of care when it comes to MOT preparation. Our MOT checklist ensures that your vehicle is not only compliant with all road safety regulations but also performing at its peak. Whether it’s your Lamborghini Huracán or Ferrari Purosangue, our technicians are trained to meet the specific demands of high-performance vehicles.

Our MOT Preparation Service Includes:

  • Comprehensive Diagnostic Check: Advanced computer diagnostics to ensure all systems are performing optimally and ECU checks.
  • Brake System Inspection: Examination of brake pads, discs, and fluid levels to maintain precision stopping power.
  • Suspension and Steering: Detailed checks on your supercar’s suspension and alignment systems.
  • Tyre Condition,Pressure and Alignment: Tyre tread, alignment and pressure checks using approved equipment.
  • Exhaust Emissions: Ensuring compliance with stringent UK emission regulations.
  • Fluids Check: Checking and topping up oil, coolant, and hydraulic fluids to factory-recommended levels.
